Town Hall 8 War Bases

Prioritize upgrading all of your air defense to level six as soon as possible because a TH8 is finally strong enough to fight off dragon attacks from other TH8 players. Once again the key to a strong air defense is to surround your air defenses with high hit point gold/elixer storages. You will also be concerned with GoWiPe attacks at TH8. The best defense against GoWiPe is to try and have as many seperate compartments to your base as possible. This slows down golems incredibly.
